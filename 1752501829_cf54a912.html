Inventory & Sales Analysis Report

June 2024 Performance Overview

Executive Summary

This report analyzes sales and inventory data for June 2024, covering 25 orders across various product categories. Key findings include:

  • Total of 25 orders processed with varying quantities
  • 5 products were returned (20% return rate) and 4 products were exchanged
  • Top return reasons: color/description mismatch, size issues, and quality concerns
  • Inventory adjustments show significant movement in certain product categories

Top Selling Products by Quantity

Insight: Swimwear and socks show the highest sales quantities, suggesting these are popular seasonal items. Consider increasing stock levels for these categories.

Daily Order Volume

Insight: Order volume fluctuates throughout the month with noticeable peaks around mid-month. Consider promotional activities during slower periods to boost sales.

Inventory Movement by Product

Insight: Swimwear and socks show the highest inventory movement. Monitor these items closely to prevent stockouts.

Current Inventory Levels

Insight: Some products have significantly higher inventory levels than others. Consider rebalancing stock to optimize working capital.

Return Reasons Breakdown

Insight: Product description mismatches (color/size) account for most returns. Improve product descriptions and sizing guides to reduce returns.

Return Rate by Product Category

Insight: Certain categories like hats and shoes have higher return rates. Investigate quality control and sizing standards for these products.

Recommendations

  1. Inventory Optimization: Increase stock levels for high-demand items like swimwear and socks while reducing overstocked items.
  2. Return Reduction: Improve product descriptions, sizing guides, and quality control to minimize returns.
  3. Promotional Strategy: Plan targeted promotions during slower sales periods to smooth demand fluctuations.
  4. Product Analysis: Investigate why certain categories (hats, shoes) have higher return rates and take corrective action.
  5. Supplier Review: For products with quality-related returns, review supplier performance and quality standards.
